\begin{frame}{Perspectives}
  Our experiements do not show a direct correlation between aspect-ratio and lossage as we predicted.

  There could be many reasons for this.

  \begin{itemize}
  \item need a better way to measure aspect ratio
  \item secondary effects of RE operations NOT/STAR have more drastic effects that our intuition indicates
  \item RE algebra has more annihilators that numerical arithmetic

  \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
